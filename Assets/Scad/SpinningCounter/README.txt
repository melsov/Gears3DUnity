# Summary

A remix of Sibanamush's  'Spinning Life Counter with Numbers' http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:538696  

Simply spin the numbers and use the indicator in the front to keep track! There is a built-in stand to keep the life counter upright during use. 

# Instructions

EDIT: At it's current scale, it's a little hard to turn the numbers. I would recommend actually printing at 1.5x or 2x scale.  

Print out the following pieces:   

1x pin.stl  
1x counter_left.stl  
1x cap_left.stl  
1x counter_right.stl  
1x cap_right.stl  

Slide the counter_left and counter_right onto the pin and snap the caps onto the sides.
